Codfish with Citruses and Creamy Pear

Ingredients (for 1-2 people)

  • 2 pieces of codfish
  • 2 pears
  • Lemon and lime zest
  • Salt and pepper

    • Steps

    1. Peel the pears and cook them in the microwave using Steam Case with tray for 4 minutes at 800W.
    2. Zest the lime and the lemon according to taste, then add salt and a bit of pepper. Sprinkle over the codfish and put everything in the Steam Case with tray.
    3. Cook for 4 minutes at 800W, on the tray, on top of the pears.
    4. Smash the pears, then put them on the serving plate as a base for the fish. Serve.
