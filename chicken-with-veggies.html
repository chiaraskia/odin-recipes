Chicken with Veggies

Ingredients (for 1-2 people)

  • 450 g Chicken breasts without bones or skin
  • 6 halves of Brussels sprouts
  • 30 ml Water
  • Half a sweet onion
  • 30 ml EV Olive Oil
  • Salt and pepper

    • Steps

    1. Put water on the bottom of the Steam Case with tray, then add the tray.
    2. Add the chicken, the onion and the Brussels sprouts on top of the tray, then add oil, salt and pepper on top.
    3. Close the Steam Case with tray and cook for 5 minutes and a half at 800W.
